How the Web Changed the World

Communication

The World Wide Web has revolutionized communication. It enables instant messaging, video calls, and social media interactions, connecting people across the globe. Tools like Google Workspace and Slack make real-time collaboration possible.

Business

Businesses have embraced the web for e-commerce, digital marketing, and remote work. Platforms like Amazon and Shopify allow companies to reach customers worldwide, while tools like Zoom and cloud services support flexible work environments.

Education

The web democratizes learning by providing access to online courses, digital libraries, and virtual classrooms. Platforms like Khan Academy and Coursera help individuals acquire new skills from anywhere in the world.

The Web's Impact on Modern Life

The World Wide Web has transformed modern life by making communication, education, business, and daily activities more accessible and convenient. From connecting people globally to streamlining everyday tasks, its impact is unparalleled
